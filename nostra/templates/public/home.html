Welcome to Finostra

Finostra is an application that creates financial models in Excel. It is designed and specialized to help professionals in the financial services industry save an immense amount of time and effort regarding financial modeling in Excel. Currently, the app can create operational, DCF, merger, and LBO models of any publicly traded company/companies required to submit their financials to the SEC.
